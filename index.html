Why Stripeflare?

Built specifically for edge computing with performance and developer experience in mind

Lightning Performance

User-segmented databases with aggregate mirroring deliver sub-20ms balance operations using Cloudflare Durable Objects.

Zero Auth Friction

No signup forms or password management. Users authenticate purely through Stripe payments – the payment IS the login.

Login by Payment

Unique feature: users can access their account from any device by making a payment with the same payment method.

One-Line Integration

Wrap your handler with withStripeflare() and you're done. No complex setup or configuration required.

Edge-Native

Built specifically for Cloudflare Workers, not adapted from traditional server architectures.

ACID Compliant

Powered by DORM with SQLite under the hood, ensuring data consistency and reliability at the edge.
